# Article Publishing Checklist

Before publishing each Nangkiew Foundation article:

- Title is clear and specific.
- Opening explains why the issue matters.
- Claims based on data or policy have a source.
- Meghalaya-specific claims are distinguished from broader Indian or global evidence.
- Recommendations are separated from factual description.
- Author names and affiliations are accurate.
- Every link works.
- References use credible primary or high-quality secondary sources.
- Article has a 1–2 sentence description for the homepage.
- Category and reading time are filled in.
- Spelling, dates and names have been proofread.
- One person other than the author has reviewed the final draft.
